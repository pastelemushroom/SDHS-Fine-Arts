SDHS Fine Arts


At Soddy Daisy High School, we believe that the arts are a vital part of learning! Rather than containing core academic lessons, SDHS's fine arts programs strive to inspire creativity in students, as well as promote critical thinking, problem solving, teamwork, speaking & communication, and of course, performance!
